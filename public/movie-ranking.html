Ranking Series I have watched

Top 3 Series

1. Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a crime drama series that follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, as he partners with former student Jesse Pinkman to secure his family's financial future after being diagnosed with terminal cancer.

2. Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama series based on George R.R. Martin's novels, set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, where noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne and the Seven Kingdoms, amidst political intrigue, betrayal, and epic battles.

3. Suits

Suits is a legal drama series that follows Mike Ross, a brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory, who lands a job as a law associate for Harvey Specter, despite never having attended law school, leading to a series of high-stakes cases and ethical dilemmas.
